Got Questions?

How do I create my playlists?

To create custom playlists, simply click on the “+” button next to a song. You can add as many tracks as you want.

Can I share my playlists?

Absolutely! Share your playlists with friends by clicking the “Share” button and copying the link.

What devices can I use?

Our music player works on desktops, smartphones, and tablets. Just sign in to your account.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime under the “Settings” tab in your account.
